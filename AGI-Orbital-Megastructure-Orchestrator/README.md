# AGI Orbital Megastructure Orchestrator

The AGI Orbital Megastructure Orchestrator represents multi-agent AI applied to civil engineering on an astronomical scale. When tasked with a massive, multi-decade goal—like assembling the first phases of a Dyson Swarm or a massive orbital habitat—the Master Orchestrator delegates the workload.

The Master Orchestrator spawns specialized sub-agents:
- **Asteroid Mining Lead:** Secures raw materials from near-Earth objects.
- **Orbital Assembly Commander:** Manages zero-g robotic construction fleets.
- **Energy Logistics Coordinator:** Routes generated power back to Earth or to the construction fleet.

These agents operate in parallel. The Master Orchestrator synthesizes their supply chain and physics constraints into a cohesive, multi-year macro-engineering blueprint.

### How it makes work easier
Human planners cannot optimize the logistics of millions of autonomous drones and billions of tons of asteroid material across decades. This AGI provides a unified, mathematically optimal project management strategy for Kardashev-scale engineering.
