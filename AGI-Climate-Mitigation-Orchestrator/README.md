# AGI Climate Mitigation Orchestrator

A leap into **Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** applied to global Climate Tech and Crisis Management.

When faced with a complex, ambiguous macro-level crisis (e.g., "A severe multi-year drought is projected to critically impact the Mediterranean basin"), the AGI Master Orchestrator takes over. It breaks down the high-level threat and spawns specialized sub-agents:
- **Climate Modeler** (analyzing weather patterns)
- **Agri-Economics** (evaluating supply chain impacts)
- **Policy & Relief** (drafting mitigation frameworks)

These agents operate simultaneously across complex domains. The Orchestrator then synthesizes their findings into a cohesive, global mitigation strategy. Features BYOK architecture support (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama).
