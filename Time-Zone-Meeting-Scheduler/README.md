# Time Zone Meeting Scheduler

## Idea Overview
The Time Zone Meeting Scheduler is a visual utility designed to solve one of the biggest headaches of remote, global teams: finding a suitable meeting time across drastically different time zones. Instead of relying on complex mental math or constantly converting times in your head, this tool provides a clear, visual overlap of the standard working hours for every participant you add.

## How it makes work easy and efficient
*   **Visual Overlap:** Instantly highlights the "golden hours" where everyone is likely to be awake and working, drastically reducing back-and-forth emails.
*   **Error Reduction:** Automates the calculation of offsets based on UTC, eliminating human error in time conversion.
*   **Team Empathy:** Makes it visually obvious who has to stay up late or wake up early for a proposed meeting, fostering better team scheduling practices.
*   **Speed:** Simply add the locations/offsets of your teammates and instantly see the available slots.

## Technologies Used
*   HTML5
*   CSS3 (Flexbox/Grid for the timeline visualization)
*   JavaScript (Vanilla) for dynamic time calculation and DOM manipulation.
