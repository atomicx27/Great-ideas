# AGI-Lunar-Base-Orchestrator
A leap into **Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** applied to Space Exploration. When a complex, ambiguous crisis occurs (e.g., "Life Support Array failure during a solar storm"), the AGI Master Orchestrator assumes control. It breaks down the high-level survival goal and spawns specialized sub-agents: Life Support (managing backup O2), Energy Management (rerouting power from non-essentials), and Communications (establishing an emergency link to Earth). These agents operate in parallel, and the Master synthesizes their outputs into a cohesive, base-wide stabilization strategy. Features BYOK architecture support.
