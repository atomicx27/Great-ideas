# AGI Oceanic Research Swarm

A multi-agent AGI system orchestrating a swarm of underwater drones, submersibles, and sensor buoys. Given a high-level research goal, the master orchestrator spawns and coordinates specialized agents that execute complex mapping, biological sampling, and chemical analysis simultaneously.

## Features
- AGI Master Orchestrator for complex goal breakdown.
- Multi-agent swarm coordination and parallel execution visualization.
- Synthesis of disparate data streams into a cohesive research report.
- BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) architecture for OpenAI, Anthropic, or Ollama.
