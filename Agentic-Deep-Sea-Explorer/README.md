# Agentic Deep Sea Explorer

## Idea
An autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) system powered by an LLM agent. It processes sonar and visual data to classify biological anomalies, adapt its lighting and sensor configurations in real-time, and plot safe trajectories through treacherous underwater caves or hydrothermal vent fields.

## How it makes work easier
Radio signals do not travel well underwater, making remote human control difficult. This agentic system operates fully autonomously, interpreting complex environments and making split-second navigational and scientific decisions without needing a constant tether to a surface ship.
