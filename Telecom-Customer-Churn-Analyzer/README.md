# Telecom Customer Churn Analyzer

This tool helps telecom customer retention teams identify accounts at high risk of churning. It analyzes usage metrics, support tickets, and overages. It features an integrated "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) architecture, allowing teams to use AI (OpenAI, Anthropic, or a local/private Ollama instance) to automatically generate highly personalized retention offers and emails based on the customer's specific grievance.
