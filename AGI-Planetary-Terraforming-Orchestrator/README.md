# AGI Planetary Terraforming Orchestrator

**Idea:** A leap into **Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** applied to macro-scale planetary engineering. Faced with the complex goal of "Make Mars habitable," the AGI Master Orchestrator breaks down the mission and spawns specialized sub-agents: Atmospheric Generators, Hydrological Melters, and Microbial Seeders.

**How it makes work easier:** It coordinates massive, interdependent global operations in parallel. The Master synthesizes their findings into a cohesive, multi-century terraforming strategy, ensuring that melting ice aligns with temperature increases and microbial seeding, avoiding ecological collapse.
