# Automated Carbon Footprint Calculator

The Automated Carbon Footprint Calculator is a classic **Automation** tool in the Climate Tech & Carbon Management domain. It is designed to replace manual environmental auditing spreadsheets.

It does not use AI or LLMs. Instead, it relies on deterministic mathematical rules and standard EPA conversion factors to calculate an enterprise's Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions based on raw usage data (kWh, Therms, Flight Miles).
