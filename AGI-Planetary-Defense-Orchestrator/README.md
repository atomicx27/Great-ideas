# AGI Planetary Defense Orchestrator

The AGI Planetary Defense Orchestrator represents the pinnacle of multi-agent AI applied to existential threat mitigation. When faced with a complex, highly ambiguous macro-goal—such as defending against a fragmented asteroid swarm with multiple global impact zones—a single AI cannot handle the interdisciplinary constraints.

The Master Orchestrator spawns specialized sub-agents:
- **Tracking Swarm Lead:** Computes thousands of trajectories simultaneously.
- **Deflection Commander:** Allocates global nuclear and kinetic assets.
- **Evacuation Coordinator:** Plans civilian displacement in predicted impact zones.

These agents operate in parallel, and the Master Orchestrator synthesizes their competing constraints into a cohesive, actionable executive defense strategy.

### How it makes work easier
It acts as the ultimate crisis management AI. Instead of disjointed international agencies arguing over resource allocation during an apocalyptic event, the AGI instantly processes billions of data points to provide the mathematically optimal strategy for global survival.
