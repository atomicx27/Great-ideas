# AGI-Precision-Medicine-Orchestrator

A hypothetical leap into **Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** within the Healthcare/Biotech domain. This multi-agent swarm orchestrator tackles the complex, highly ambiguous goal of designing a personalized treatment plan for a patient with a rare genetic condition.

The Master Orchestrator breaks down the patient profile and spawns specialized sub-agents:
- **Genomics Expert:** Analyzes the genetic variants.
- **Pharmacologist:** Checks drug interactions and efficacies against the genetic profile.
- **Oncology/Specialty Expert:** Synthesizes disease-specific treatment protocols.

These agents operate in parallel, and the Master synthesizes their outputs into a cohesive, holistic Precision Medicine Strategy. Features BYOK architecture support.
