# AGI Ocean City Orchestrator

## Idea
An overarching AI governor system for managing a floating or submerged oceanic city. It coordinates specialized agents for structural ballasting, desalination output scaling, and oceanic traffic control to ensure the city remains stable, supplied, and safe during extreme weather events.

## How it makes work easier
A floating city presents complex, dynamically interacting systems that are too fast-moving for human operators during a storm. The AGI governor instantly calculates trade-offs between stability, power usage, and supply needs, keeping the metropolis functioning flawlessly.
